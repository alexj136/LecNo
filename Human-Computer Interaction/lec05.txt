Establishing requirements
=========================

Understand context, users & task
  Conduct user research
  Market research

Requirements arise from understanding of user's needs

Functional requirements: What a system should do (or should not do)

Non-functional: How the system should be
  Constraints or qualities
  Anything that isn't a functional requirement
  Performance - speed, data usage etc
  Scalability
  Environmental:
    Dusty/noisy/bright/dark/heat etc
  Social: privacy, sharing etc
  Technical ability of user

Stakeholder involvement
  Stakeholder: Anyone affected by the system or its development
  Users - primary stakeholders
  Developers, deployers - secondary
  Financial benefactors, company shareholders etc - tertiary

Methods
  Personas
  Scenarios
  Use caases
  Heirarchical task analysis
